JULY

July is the seventh month of the year in the Gregorian calendar and is typically associated with summer in the Northern Hemisphere and winter in the Southern Hemisphere. Named after Julius Caesar, July often features warm and sunny weather, making it a popular time for vacations, outdoor activities, and festivals. Significant events in July include Independence Day in the United States on July 4th and Bastille Day in France on July 14th. It's a month of vibrant energy and celebration.
